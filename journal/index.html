Sun, 20 Oct 2025, 13:14 - Home, London

There was a time when we could spend days, if not weeks, perfecting the tiniest design details.

Kerning headline, testing countless fonts, perfecting the speed of every interactions and more...

That luxury is gone!

In the age of AI, speed is everything!

Products, features we design evolve over night.

The window between idea, design and development is shorter than ever.

If your design process can't keep up, you won't be able to contribute to AI innovations!

But, moving fast doesn't mean lowering the standards.

It doesn't mean not-caring for the details.

It means focusing on what truly matters!

The parts that shapes perception, usability, clarity, trust...

And letting go of everything else that doesn't!

Speed is not about rushing!

Speed is about intent and focus!

Adopting this mindset has played a big role in the success of the products I've designed with AI founders.

I design fast because the AI world move fast.

And we slow down only to perfect what truly matters.
